About Me

My name is Jake Cioffi and I have been designing content for the last two years while studying graphic design. Originally a fan of being in front of the camera, I decided to take my talents behind one in order to explore the depth of my creative imagination.

Background

I grew up in Long Island, New York and went to Chaminade High School which later allowed me to attend James Madison University in Harrisonburg, Virginia. Here, I began exploring different career paths, and eventually I landed on interactive graphic design.

Proficiency

This web site is designed as a portfolio of my work I have completed over the course of two semesters at JMU. In completing this, I have continued learning about the functionality and use of multiple different applications and coding languages (CSS, HTML, JS, Bootstrap)
